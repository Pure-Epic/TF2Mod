TF2Mod is a Terraria content mod that is based on Valve's hit class-based shooter, Team Fortress 2. The mod adds a new player type, called mercenaries, that plays differently from your typical Terrarian.

[h1]Features:[/h1]
[list]
[*]Mercenaries which can be created within the mercenary creation menu when creating a new player.
[*]Nine new classes with their own special abilities.
[*]Weapons that can reload and support dynamic damage scalings.
[*]The Mann Co. Store with its own special currency.
[*]Enemy mercenaries that drop money upon defeat.
[*]Support for bullet hell battles.
[*]An experience that no other mod can provide.
[/list]
