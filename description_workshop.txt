TF2Mod is a pretty cool mod, it is one of the Terraria mods ever.

[h1]Features:[/h1]
[list]
[*]Weapons from TF2
[*]NPCs
[*]Accessories
[*][strike]Tools to help destroy Gensokyo[/strike]
[/list]

[h1]Future Features:[/h1]
[list]
[*]More weapons
[*]More bosses
[/list]

[h1]Cross Mod Content:[/h1]
[list]
[*]Adds post-Moon Lord progression for the Calamity Mod.
[*]Has incredible synergy with the Gensokyo mod. It's highly recommended to combine both mods together.
[/list]
